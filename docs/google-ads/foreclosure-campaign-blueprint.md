## Conversion Tracking Setup

**Primary conversion:** Form submit on `/foreclosure-deals-vancouver`
**Conversion action:** Existing Lead (Form Submit) — `QvTFCLiluvsbEIOZuKtA` (shared with developer-deals; UTM `utm_campaign=foreclosure` differentiates attribution in reporting)

**Secondary conversion:** Phone call from ad (track in Google Ads Phone Call Conversion)

**Required UTM params on every Final URL:**
- `utm_source=google`
- `utm_medium=cpc`
- `utm_campaign=foreclosure`
- `utm_content={ad-group-shorthand}-rsa{N}`
- `utm_term={keyword}` (Google Ads keyword insertion)

**Required offline conversion path:** GHL workflow logs the source/UTM at lead creation. After 30 days, sync any closed/won opportunities back to Google Ads as **offline conversions** via the `CRM Lead (Offline)` action (per the MEMORY.md hard rule on conversion tracking). Without this, 20–40% of conversion data is lost to ad blockers.

## UTM Parameter Reference

For any cross-team work, UTMs from this campaign decode as:

```
utm_source=google           → traffic from Google Search
utm_medium=cpc              → paid search clicks
utm_campaign=foreclosure    → this campaign (vs. developer-deals, medical-strata)
utm_content=<group>-rsa<N>  → which ad group / which RSA variant
utm_term={keyword}          → the specific keyword that triggered the click
```

In GHL contact records, these populate the contact's source attribution and route to the appropriate nurture workflow.
