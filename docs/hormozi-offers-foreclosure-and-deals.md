# Grand-Slam Offers — Foreclosure + Developer Deals

This doc constructs **Money Model offer stacks** (Attraction → Upsell → Downsell → Continuity) for two new 88West landing pages: Greater Vancouver foreclosures and developer-exclusive condo inventory. Both serve dual audiences (investors + owner-occupiers) — each stage notes how the offer flexes between the two segments. Constraints obey the brokerage scope: MLS access, buyer-agency, offer writing, financing referrals, conveyance coordination. Nothing requires 88West to be a lender, manager, or contractor.

## Foreclosure Deals — Money Model Stack

### Stage I — Attraction Offer (lead magnet)

**Name:** **"The Vancouver Foreclosure Sheet"** — Greater Vancouver Court-Ordered Sales, Updated Daily Before MLS Sees Them

**Tagline (hero / above-fold):** *Every court-ordered, power-of-sale, and bank-listed property in Greater Vancouver — landing in your inbox at 7 AM, before your buyer's agent even logs in.*

**What's IN it (specifics — these are the columns):**
1. **Property address + neighborhood + property type** (detached / townhouse / condo / multi-fam)
2. **Court file number + court date** (when the offer schedule is set)
3. **Listing status** (newly listed / accepting offers / under court approval / pending)
4. **List price + last bank-appraised value + last assessed value** (BC Assessment) — so the spread is visible
5. **Days on market + price reductions to date**
6. **Building / lot specs** — beds, baths, sqft, year built, lot size
7. **Condition flag** — owner-occupied / vacant / tenanted / boarded — flagged from drive-by + listing notes
8. **Comp anchor** — last 3 comparable sales within 500m / 90 days, $/sqft delta vs subject
9. **Estimated bring-current cost band** — $0-15K / $15-50K / $50K+ for cosmetic-to-structural (ballpark)
10. **Offer mechanics flag** — "Schedule A required," "subject-free preferred," "lender will counter," "court approval needed" — translated into plain English
11. **Investor metric block** — gross yield % at current rents (CMHC market rents), 5-yr appreciation comp, all-in carry estimate at current 5-yr fixed
12. **Owner-occupier metric block** — total monthly cost at 20% down + current rate + property tax + strata vs comparable rental in same building/area
13. **Risk flag** — rescission risk, redemption period status, occupant-removal risk

**Cadence:** Daily 7 AM PT email digest. Weekly Saturday "movers" recap (price drops, court-date changes, newly redeemed). Real-time push for **subscriber-set criteria matches** ($X budget, area, type) within 1 hour of new listing.

**Delivery mechanism (tiered, friction-laddered):**
- **Tier 0 — Email digest** (default): PDF attachment + summary table. Email-only opt-in.
- **Tier 1 — Subscriber dashboard** (after phone verify): live filterable table at `go.88westrealty.com/foreclosures/dashboard` with saved searches and historical price-drop tracking.
- **Tier 2 — Tour-ready alerts** (after a 5-min qualification call): SMS push within 1 hour of any criteria match + same-day showing scheduling.

**Form ask (progressive — not all upfront):**
- **Initial form** (above-fold): email + first name + budget range (5 buckets: <$700K / $700K-1M / $1-1.5M / $1.5-2.5M / $2.5M+) + intent ("I'm investing" / "I'm buying to live in" / "I'm exploring") + areas (multi-select: Vancouver / North Van / Burnaby / Richmond / Other).
- **After download** (thank-you page): "Want it before MLS? Verify your phone for early-access alerts" → SMS verify + optional financing-pre-approval status dropdown.
- **Later (drip email day 3):** "Tell us your buyer profile" — 60-second quiz that segments investor vs owner-occupier and triggers the right nurture sequence.

**Conditional "Win Your Money Back" guarantee equivalent (since the lead magnet is free):**

> **The 30-Day Tour Guarantee:** *If our daily Foreclosure Sheet doesn't surface at least one Greater Vancouver property worth touring in your budget within 30 days, our broker will personally call you and walk you through 3 off-market alternatives we're sitting on — and waive the buyer-agency engagement fee on any deal that comes from that call.*

This is the conditional upgrade Hormozi describes. It tilts likelihood (we're putting our compensation on the line), removes time-delay anxiety (30-day clock), and pre-qualifies for intent (only people serious enough to want a guarantee bother to read it).

**Value Equation analysis:**

| Lever | How this offer tilts it |
|---|---|
| **Dream Outcome** | "Buy a Vancouver property below market — before everyone else sees it." Investors hear ROI. Owner-occupiers hear "the home I couldn't otherwise afford." Both dreams sit on the same data feed. |
| **Perceived Likelihood** | Concrete columns (court file #, BC Assessment delta, comp anchor) prove the data is real. Daily cadence proves operational discipline. The 30-day guarantee transfers risk from prospect to brokerage. Add 3 testimonial logos ("Bought $1.4M North Van home for $1.18M via court sale") near the form. |
| **Time Delay** | Free, instant download → first list in inbox same day. Tier 2 alert subscribers get matches inside 1 hour of listing. |
| **Effort & Sacrifice** | Email-only opt-in for tier 0 (15 sec to get in). Comp anchors + cost bands + plain-English offer mechanics mean the prospect doesn't have to decode legalese, MLS data, or BC Assessment portals themselves. We did the synthesis. |

### Stage II — Upsell

**Name:** **"Foreclosure Buyer Concierge — Court-to-Keys"**

**What it is:** A premium full buyer-agency engagement specifically built around foreclosure mechanics, with bonuses that ordinary buyer-agency contracts don't include.

**What's included:**
1. **Dedicated foreclosure broker** assigned within 24 hrs of signing — one of 88West's 3 specialists who has personally closed 10+ court-ordered sales
2. **Same-day showing access** for any Sheet listing the client flags
3. **Schedule A drafting** + pre-flight legal review with a partnered Vancouver real-estate lawyer (lawyer fees still client's, but the intro and first 30-min consult bundled)
4. **Court date attendance** — broker physically attends Master's Chambers approval hearings on client's behalf
5. **Pre-approval acceleration** — direct intro to 3 mortgage brokers who underwrite court-sale subject-frees
6. **Inspection coordination** during the (often <48 hr) inspection window with vetted Vancouver inspectors
7. **Renovation cost validation** — a single complimentary on-site visit from a partnered contractor (e.g., Brio) to ballpark bring-current cost before you bid
8. **"First Look" — 24-hour exclusivity** on any new Sheet listing the client has flagged in their saved search before it's released to other Sheet subscribers

**Pricing / commitment:** Standard buyer-agency commission (paid by seller in most BC court sales — broker fee terms disclosed upfront). **Plus** a **$500 fully-refundable Search Activation Fee** that is refunded at closing OR if the client and broker mutually agree there's no fit within 60 days.

**Conditional guarantee (Hormozi "Win Your Money Back" pattern):**
> **The 90-Day Bid Guarantee:** *If we don't help you submit at least 3 qualified offers on Greater Vancouver foreclosures within 90 days of signing, we refund your $500 Activation Fee AND keep working without it for another 90 days.*

**Value Equation tilt:**
| Lever | Tilt |
|---|---|
| **Dream** | "Close a foreclosure in 90 days, with a specialist who has done it 10+ times, without losing the deal to a Schedule A error." |
| **Likelihood** | Specialist credentialing + lawyer + lender + inspector network = a system, not a pitch. Conditional refund on activation fee. |
| **Time** | First Look 24-hr exclusivity collapses the discovery-to-tour timeline. Court-date attendance removes a coordination delay. |
| **Effort** | Broker drafts Schedule A, attends court, coordinates inspector, intros lender. Client just decides yes/no on each property. |

**Why it's a real upsell (not a rebrand):** standard buyer-agency in BC doesn't include court attendance, Schedule A drafting coordination, contractor walk-through, or first-look exclusivity. Each of those is operationally extra and uniquely valuable for foreclosure context.

### Stage III — Downsell

**Name:** **"Foreclosure Match — Pro"** (paid subscription, light-touch)

**For whom:** A prospect who downloads the free Sheet but isn't ready to sign a buyer-agency yet. Maybe they're 6-12 months out, maybe they want to see the data harder before committing, maybe they live abroad and aren't in market position yet.

**What's included:**
1. Everything in the free Sheet **plus** real-time push alerts (vs daily digest)
2. **Custom buy-box** — broker-curated saved search refined via 1 onboarding call (30 min)
3. **Monthly 30-min strategy call** with an 88West broker (recorded, sent as MP3)
4. **Comp deep-dives on demand** — up to 5/month: send any Sheet listing, get a 1-page valuation memo back within 48 hrs
5. **Foreclosure 101 video library** access (10-12 videos: how court sales work, redemption periods, tenant rights, financing nuances, owner-occupier red flags, investor ROI math)
6. **Exit ramp:** 100% of subscription fees paid in the prior 6 months are credited toward buyer-agency engagement when client converts to the upsell

**Pricing:** **$49/month** (or **$490/year** — annual saves 17%). Cancel anytime.

**Why this works as a downsell:** small commitment, low price ($49 < a Vancouver dinner), high information value, and the **6-month credit-back** mechanic actively pulls subscribers toward the upsell. It also creates a **paid intent signal** — a $49/mo subscriber has demonstrated 49× more intent than a free-list email opt-in, which makes the broker's prioritization easy.

**Value Equation tilt:**
| Lever | Tilt |
|---|---|
| **Dream** | "I'm building toward a foreclosure purchase in the next 6-12 months and want a specialist's brain on retainer." |
| **Likelihood** | The credit-back mechanic guarantees fees aren't wasted — every dollar paid converts to closing-side value. |
| **Time** | Real-time push (vs daily digest) means the subscriber acts inside the same hour as the listing. |
| **Effort** | Broker does the comp memos, runs the strategy call agenda, curates the buy-box. Subscriber just consumes. |

### Stage IV — Continuity

**Name:** **"88West Off-Market Alpha"** (post-purchase club for clients who've already closed at least one deal)

**Pricing:** **$99/month** or **$999/year** (15% annual discount — same pricing logic Brio uses for Annual Home Care and that benchmarks well against TruBlue / Kincaid). Past clients only — invitation-tier.

**What's included:**
1. **Off-market deal flow** — 3-5 properties per month that 88West's broker network sources before MLS (probate, divorce, distressed-but-not-yet-court, tired-landlord, builder-overflow). Sent as a private PDF.
2. **Quarterly portfolio review** (45-min Zoom): broker pulls BC Assessment changes + comparable sales + rental yield drift on every property the client owns and produces a 1-page equity report.
3. **Annual Home Equity Report** — full 5-year forward projection on each owned property: appreciation comp, tax assessment trajectory, refi-equity-extraction window, and a "should I sell?" signal score.
4. **Refinance / equity-extraction triggers** — alerts when the math shifts (e.g., assessment up 12% and 5-yr fixed dropped 50bps).
5. **Priority access** to any future buyer-agency engagement — no Search Activation Fee on second and subsequent purchases.
6. **Referral concierge** — a dedicated point of contact for any friend/family referrals + a $1,500 referral fee paid on closed deals.
7. **Quarterly investor-only or owner-occupier-only mastermind dinner** (rotates by Vancouver / North Van / Burnaby / Richmond) — networking + market briefing.

**Why this works (operational + economic):**
- Real estate brokerages historically lose the client between transactions. The Off-Market Alpha gives 88West a **structural reason to stay in the inbox** for 5-10 years between purchase and sale, which is when referrals happen.
- **Off-market deal flow** is the highest-perceived-value benefit — and 88West is uniquely positioned to source it from their own network, with marginal incremental cost.
- **$99/month is the "pocket-change ceiling"** Hormozi/Kincaid benchmark, frictionless to keep paying.
- **Quarterly review + equity report = data-driven retention** — every quarter the client gets concrete proof of the membership's value.
- For investors specifically, the off-market flow is a **direct competitive moat** vs MLS-only buyer-agencies.

**Value Equation tilt:**
| Lever | Tilt |
|---|---|
| **Dream** | "I have a broker on retainer who tells me when to buy, hold, refi, or sell — and the deals I'd never see otherwise come to me first." |
| **Likelihood** | Past-client-only positioning means every member already trusts the brand. Quarterly tangible deliverable (equity report) keeps the membership tangible. |
| **Time** | Real-time off-market alerts. Equity report quarterly — fast cadence vs traditional broker check-ins (annual or never). |
| **Effort** | Member receives PDFs and Zoom invites — zero friction to consume. |

### Optional Stage-Zero Attractors (warm-up offers — pre-qualify higher-intent leads)

Sometimes the BEST first touch is NOT the daily list. Two that should run in parallel:

**Zero-A: "Vancouver Foreclosure ROI Calculator + Report"**
- Interactive calculator at `/foreclosure-roi-calculator` — input price, area, beds, intended use (rental / flip / live-in)
- Outputs estimated rent, all-in carry cost at current 5-yr fixed, gross + net yield, 5-yr appreciation projection vs comparable non-distressed buy
- Ends with a downloadable PDF report personalized to their inputs + a soft CTA ("Want our broker to validate this on a real listing? Book a 15-min call")
- **Why it pre-qualifies:** anyone who fills it in for a $1.5M property has shown specific budget + intent. Investor-skewing.

**Zero-B: "Vancouver Foreclosure Risk Checklist (for owner-occupiers)"**
- 12-question PDF: "Is this foreclosure safe to live in?" — covers redemption-period risk, tenant occupation, bank-vs-court process, condition red flags, financing risk, neighbourhood considerations
- Free PDF download for an email
- **Why it pre-qualifies:** owner-occupier-skewing (which is the harder, more anxious segment). The download itself addresses the "is this a scam?" objection that kills the segment, and the email follow-up sequence can lean educational.

These two together segment investor vs owner-occupier intent **before** the lead lands in the main Sheet funnel — letting the email nurture immediately speak the right language.

## Developer Condo Deals — Money Model Stack

> **Hormozi-fit angle (recommendation):** **"The $100K Discount"** is the strongest Hormozi anchor — it's directly value-anchored ("dream outcome × proof"), specific, falsifiable, and lets you put a literal dollar figure in every line of copy. **"The Window"** (urgency) and **"The $43K Head Start"** (first-time-buyer-specific) layer beautifully on top as wrappers but don't carry the offer alone. Build the Money Model on $100K Discount; use The Window as the time-pressure wrapper and $43K Head Start as the first-time-buyer-specific landing-page variant.

### Stage I — Attraction Offer (lead magnet)

**Name:** **"The Developer Incentive Tracker"** — Every Builder Concession on Greater Vancouver New Construction, Updated Daily

**Tagline:** *We track every price drop, free-parking add-on, mortgage buy-down, and decorating-allowance from 60+ Vancouver-area developers. You read it Monday morning. You decide which $100K window to walk through.*

**What's IN it (the columns):**
1. **Project name + developer + neighborhood + completion stage** (pre-sale / under construction / move-in ready / standing inventory)
2. **Original list price + current price + total $ off** (the headline number for the wrapper)
3. **Stacked incentives** itemized:
   - Decorating allowance ($)
   - Free parking / storage ($ value)
   - Mortgage rate buy-down (basis points × term × notional → $ value)
   - Bonus suite-finish upgrades ($ value)
   - Closing-cost coverage ($)
   - Strata-fee holiday (months × monthly fee → $ value)
   - GST + PTT rebate eligibility flag
4. **Total incentive package $** (this is the $100K Discount headline)
5. **Unit availability** — number of units left at this incentive level + which floor plans
6. **Comp price band** — what comparable resale (within 1km, last 6 mo) is closing at; spread vs. list-after-incentive
7. **Estimated monthly cost** — at 20% down, current 5-yr fixed, including strata & taxes
8. **Investor block** — projected rent (CMHC + market analog), gross + net yield, cash-on-cash at 20% down
9. **First-time-buyer block** — eligible programs, how the developer incentives stack with provincial rebates ($43K Head Start framing)
10. **Window flag** — "expires when X units sold" / "until end of month" / "until project hits 70% sold" — this powers The Window urgency angle

**Cadence:** Monday 7 AM PT digest. Mid-week flash alert when a project changes incentive structure or drops a unit. Real-time SMS alert (tier 2) on subscriber-set criteria match.

**Delivery mechanism (mirrors foreclosure tier structure):**
- Tier 0: Email digest + PDF
- Tier 1: Subscriber dashboard with filters (price, neighborhood, beds, completion date, incentive size)
- Tier 2: SMS alerts after phone verify

**Form ask:**
- **Initial:** email + first name + budget bucket + intent (investor / owner-occupier / first-time buyer) + completion timeframe (next 6 mo / 6-18 mo / 18+ mo)
- **Thank-you page:** phone verify for SMS alerts + first-time buyer status flag (unlocks $43K Head Start variant content)

**Conditional guarantee:**
> **The Incentive-Match Promise:** *If the Tracker doesn't surface at least one Greater Vancouver developer offer matching your budget and timeline within 30 days, our broker personally walks you through every project where 88West has off-the-record builder relationships and pre-negotiates a builder-direct concession on your behalf — at no charge.*

**Value Equation analysis:**

| Lever | Tilt |
|---|---|
| **Dream Outcome** | "$100K off a brand-new Vancouver condo, with the developer paying my legal and decorating costs, and a rate buy-down on top." Specific, falsifiable, dollar-anchored. |
| **Likelihood** | Itemized incentive columns + total $ = irrefutable. Every entry is a public-record builder offer. The Tracker is a synthesis layer on data the prospect could find themselves but never would. |
| **Time** | Free instant download. Mid-week flash alerts collapse new-incentive-to-buyer time. |
| **Effort** | Builder by builder, project by project, incentive-stacking is exhausting to track manually. The Tracker centralizes it + translates the $ value of every non-cash incentive (which buyers consistently underestimate). |

**What it is:** Full buyer-agency for new construction with three differentiators ordinary buyer-agencies don't offer.

**What's included:**

**Pricing:** Standard buyer-agency commission (typically paid by developer in BC pre-sales — confirm per project). **Plus** a **$500 fully-refundable Search Activation Fee** refunded at closing OR within 60 days if no fit.

## Cross-cutting recommendations

### Which approved developer-deals angle best fits a Hormozi launch?

**Recommendation: lead with "The $100K Discount"** as the primary Money Model anchor. It's the only one of the three approved angles that is directly **dollar-anchored**, **provable per-listing**, and **applicable to both audiences** (investor sees it as ROI; owner-occupier sees it as affordability). The other two angles become **wrappers** layered on top, not separate funnels:

- **The $100K Discount** = the offer. The Tracker, the Negotiator upsell, and every CTA quantify the discount.
- **The Window** = the time-pressure **wrapper** (Hormozi's wrapper-vs-offer distinction). It powers the urgency layer in copy: "Incentives expire when 70% of units sell." Use it on retargeting and mid-funnel sequences, not the cold-traffic primary hero.
- **The $43K Head Start** = the **first-time-buyer landing-page variant**. Same Tracker, same Negotiator, but the hero copy + Zero-B calculator + email nurture lean into rebate-stacking, not investor ROI. This becomes a separate landing page (`/first-time-buyer-vancouver-presale`) sharing the same backend funnel.

This gives you one Money Model with three creative wrappers — exactly the wrapper-vs-offer architecture Hormozi prescribes (test 10-50 wrappers per offer, change the offer rarely).

### What 88West needs to operationally produce these offers

**Data feeds:**
- **Foreclosure data:** automated daily scrape of BC Supreme Court filings + power-of-sale listings on MLS + a manual broker adds layer for off-MLS bank listings. ~10-15 hrs/week of analyst time (ideally a junior). This is the single biggest operational dependency.
- **Developer incentive data:** weekly check-ins with each of ~60 builders' sales centers + scrape of public price sheets. ~6-8 hrs/week. Easier than foreclosure because builder relationships already exist.
- **Comp + BC Assessment integration:** off-the-shelf via REBGV API + BCA public data. 1-time engineering build.
- **Daily email pipeline:** Mailchimp / Customer.io / Klaviyo — straightforward.
- **Subscriber dashboard:** lightweight Next.js dashboard auth-gated by Tier (1 = email-only login, 2 = phone-verified). Shared across both products.

**Email sequences (need to be built before launch):**
- **Foreclosure investor sequence:** 7-day welcome → ROI math focus → upsell pitch on day 14
- **Foreclosure owner-occupier sequence:** 7-day welcome → risk education + reassurance → upsell pitch on day 21
- **Developer-deals investor sequence:** 7-day welcome → $100K Discount math + pre-sale assignment economics → upsell on day 14
- **Developer-deals first-time-buyer sequence:** 7-day welcome → $43K rebate stack education → upsell on day 21

**Broker capacity:**
- Need at least 2 designated foreclosure specialists and 2 designated pre-sale specialists for the upsell tier (cross-trained ideally; same humans can do both)
- Off-Market Alpha continuity needs a single dedicated broker (could be the team lead) for quarterly review calls

**Tracking + CRM:**
- The existing GHL setup (used for `medical-office-for-sale-north-vancouver`) is fully reusable — clone-per-campaign per Brio playbook (see `ghl-workflow-copy-paste.md`)
- Webhook + custom fields: budget bucket, intent (investor/owner-occupier/FTB), area multi-select, completion timeframe, financing pre-approval status

**Conversion AI:**
- The GHL Conversation AI agent (already speced for Brio) can be cloned and re-prompted for foreclosure-and-deals tone. Prompt should explicitly handle the investor-vs-owner-occupier branching question early in the conversation.

1. **Foreclosure cold-traffic hero:** *"The Vancouver Foreclosures Your Buyer's Agent Doesn't Want You to See — Court-Ordered Sales, Updated Daily, Free."*
2. **Foreclosure CTA button:** *"Send Me Tomorrow's List →"* (urgency + ownership wording)
