## Key Market Data (from Perplexity Deep Research)

- 2,500-5,458 unsold condos in Metro Van — 24-year high
- Presale sales collapsed 98%: Q1 2026 = 124 units vs Q1 2021 = 6,000
- Feb 2026: only 64 presale homes launched (6% of normal ~1,100)
- Condo benchmark $704,600 — down 5.9% YoY
- Burnaby $/SF dropped 10.5% in <1 year ($780 → $698)
- Developer incentives: 5% deposits, $25K-$50K credits, free parking ($40K-$80K), zero assignment fees, vendor take-back mortgages
- Stacked incentive value: $100K+ on a $700K unit (14%+ effective discount)
- Most aggressive incentive environment since 2018
- Rental vacancy 3.7% — highest since 1988. Rents down 9.2% YoY
- Bank of Canada rate 2.25% (down 275bp from 5% peak)
- First-time buyer savings: GST rebate ~$35K + PTT exemption ~$8K = $43K on $700K
- Sales-to-active ratio 10.3% for condos (buyer's market threshold: <12%)
- BCREA: 3% growth in 2026, potential 27% jump by 2032
- CREA: 5.1% sales increase forecast for 2026, BC leading recovery
- Project cancellations: Atmosphere (Richmond), Westgroup 204-unit River District
- Canada population declined Q4 2025 (-103,504 people) — first time in years
