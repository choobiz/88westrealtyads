# Keyword Matrix: Vancouver Developer Condo Deals — Campaign 2

> Last updated: 2026-04-09
> Research method: Perplexity Deep Research MCP + Firecrawl competitive analysis
> Campaign angles: "The Window", "The $100K Discount", "The $43K Head Start"

## Strategy Overview

The presale condo market has collapsed 98% (124 units in Q1 2026 vs 6,000 in Q1 2021). This creates a keyword opportunity: generic presale keywords have lower competition than normal, while deal/incentive keywords are emerging with high intent and low saturation. Our strategy focuses on capturing:
- **Deal-seekers** searching for developer incentives and discounts (Angle 2)
- **First-time buyers** researching government programs + affordability (Angle 5)
- **Opportunistic buyers** researching the market window (Angle 1)

**Budget split:** 60% on Tier 1 transactional keywords, 25% on Tier 2 commercial, 15% on Tier 3 long-tail/awareness.
